Brand guide

The Theonix identity.

A reference for keeping Theonix OS materials consistent — logo, color, type, components, motion and voice. Use these building blocks to make anything that represents the project feel like it belongs.

01 — Logo

The mark

The Theonix logo is the primary signature of the brand. Give it room to breathe and keep it intact.

On dark surface

On light surface

Do

  • Keep clear space around the mark equal to at least the height of the logo's core shape.
  • Use the logo at a legible size on high-contrast backgrounds.
  • Use the provided logo.svg so it stays crisp at any scale.

Don't

  • Don't stretch, squash or rotate the logo.
  • Don't recolor it or apply gradients outside the brand palette.
  • Don't add drop shadows, glows, outlines or other effects to the mark itself.

05 — Surfaces & motion

Depth and movement

The interface leans on layered translucency and restrained motion to feel modern without getting in the way.

Glassmorphism surfaces

Cards and panels use blurred, semi-transparent backgrounds with a hairline border, so content floats over the space backdrop.

Glow shadows

Cyan-tinted glows and soft drop shadows give primary elements a sense of light and lift on interaction.

Respectful animation

Reveal-on-scroll, floats and transitions are subtle and purposeful — and all of it honors prefers-reduced-motion, collapsing to no motion when a visitor asks for it.
